Description
Villa with swimming pool offering a magnificent 180 ° sea view of the islands d'Or to the peninsula of Saint Mandrier via the port of Carqueiranne.

Facing south, all the rooms have a superb view of the sea, islands, the peninsula of Giens.

She has :
- a living room opening onto a long balcony,
- a dinning room,
- an open kitchen equipped with a dishwasher, an oven, a combined microwave oven, induction hob,
- 3 bedrooms including 2 bedrooms each with 1 bed of 140 * 190 and 1 bedroom with 2 beds of 80 * 190. The 3 bedrooms are air conditioned and equipped with large wardrobes,
- 1 bathroom with double sink, bath / shower, dryer towel, washing machine and dryer,
- 1 shower room communicating with 1 bedroom,
- 2 independent toilets.

Garden with a patio with plancha, communicating with the house, and a swimming pool also enjoying a sea view panoramic.
Very calm environment.

Warning: House not recommended for young children (stairs)

Located in Carqueiranne (83320), in the Mont des oiseaux, the house is a short drive from the famous white sand beach Almanarre with nautical activities (kite, paddle, board sail). The port of Hyères is 10 minutes away for excursions to the islands of Porquerolles and Port Cros. The small marina de Carqueiranne, and the city center are also 5 minutes away.
The house is 10 minutes from Toulon / Hyères airport and 10 minutes from Hyères TGV station
Our rental rates (excluding tourist taxes)

The house is designed for 6 people and the prices are for an occupancy of 6 people maximum.
Bed linen, towels and kitchen towels are provided.
All costs are included (electricity, water, internet) as well as end of stay cleaning.

In the event of a rental for several weeks, the price is reduced by € 100 per week.
